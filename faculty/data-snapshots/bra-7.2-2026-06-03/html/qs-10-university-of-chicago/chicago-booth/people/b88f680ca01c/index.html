Past Faculty Research Awards and Honors

Celebrating excellence and achievement among our faculty members.

Booth is committed to honoring the exceptional work of our faculty.

Their expertise is acknowledged through notable awards spanning a variety of disciplines, including accounting, economics, finance, the social sciences, and beyond.

Explore past accolades and achievements that highlight the impact and excellence of our faculty community.

Steven Neil Kaplan

Selected as the next fellow of the American Finance Association.

Steven Neil Kaplan

Marianne Bertrand

Selected as a 2025 Citation Laureate in Economics for her research on racial discrimination and corporate governance.

Marianne Bertrand

Alex Imas

Winner of the 2024 Dimensional Fund Advisors Prize from the Journal of Finance for his paper “Selling Fast and Buying Slow.”

Alex Imas

Killian Huber

Winner of the 2024 Moskowitz Prize, which recognizes high-impact research in sustainable finance.

Killian Huber

Pietro Veronesi and Lubos Pastor

Awarded the Stephen A. Ross Prize in Financial Economics for their paper “Technological Revolutions and Stock Prices.”

Pietro Veronesi and Lubos Pastor

Christian Leuz

Winner of the 2024 Falling Walls award for his research tackling the environmental impact of hydraulic fracturing.

Christian Leuz

Erik Hurst

Elected to the American Academy of Arts & Sciences in 2024.

Erik Hurst

Kilian Huber

Awarded a 2024 Sloan Research Fellowship. The two-year fellowships include a $75,000 cash award and honor early-career researchers.

Kilian Huber

Luigi Zingales

Elected to the American Academy of Sciences and Letters.

Luigi Zingales

Amir Sufi

Elected to the American Academy of Arts & Sciences in 2024.

Amir Sufi

Veronika Rockova

Winner of the 2024 Presidents’ Award from the Committee of Presidents of Statistical Societies for her outstanding contributions to the profession of statistics.

Veronika Rockova

Marianne Bertrand

Winner of the 2024 Barry Prize from the American Academy of Sciences and Letters.

Marianne Bertrand
