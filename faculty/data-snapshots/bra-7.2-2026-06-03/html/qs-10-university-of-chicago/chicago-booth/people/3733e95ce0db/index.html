Research Workshops Finance Workshop

In this workshop, advanced topics in finance are discussed in detail and finance research topics are presented. Faculty from other universities are invited to speak. Workshop papers will be posted below when available. Occasionally speakers will choose not to circulate their papers. In such cases, the link will be unavailable.

For information or to be added to the mailing list for weekly updates, email Toni Van House
