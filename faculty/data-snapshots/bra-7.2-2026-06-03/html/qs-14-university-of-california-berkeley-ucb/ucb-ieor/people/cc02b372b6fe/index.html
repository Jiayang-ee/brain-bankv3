Diana Chavez

Director of Career Services, Master of Analytics

Master of Analytics Career Services: manalyticscareer@berkeley.edu

Dr. Diana Chavez leads professional engagement and development initiatives for the Master of Analytics program. They design and implement comprehensive resources, programming, and group or individual sessions to help students prepare to launch their careers. In addition, Dr. Chavez helps build employer and industry relationships to connect students with internships and professional opportunities.

Josh Smothers

Assistant Director of Career Services & Employer Engagement, Master of Analytics

Master of Analytics Career Services: manalyticscareer@berkeley.edu

As the Assistant Director of Career Services & Employer Engagement, Josh facilitates connections between students and the job market by overseeing a team focused on career counseling, resume development, and interview preparation. Josh collaborates with employers to create partnerships that enhance internship and job opportunities as well as to organize events such as career fairs to encourage networking.

Outside of work, Josh spends time with his family, drawing, playing guitar, and rowing.

Jill Fujisaki

Department Manager
jillfujisaki@berkeley.edu

The Department Manager reports to the Chair and is the highest-ranking non-academic administrator in the Department and is responsible for performing the highest level of administrative and policy analysis on a broad range of issues for the Chair. Independently responsible for its overall administration, the Manager provides leadership and management direction within Industrial Engineering & Operations Research and is accountable for the following major areas: Managerial Leadership, Strategic Planning, Budget & Finance, Academic Administration (including Student Services), Technical & Instructional Support (including IT), Human Resources & Administrative Management, Space & Facilities, and Special Programs.
