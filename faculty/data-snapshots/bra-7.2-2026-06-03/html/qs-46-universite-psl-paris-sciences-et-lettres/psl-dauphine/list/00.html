    Both university and "grande école",

    Université Paris Dauphine-PSL is an institution of higher education with an international scope, specialized in the organizational and decision sciences. 

    We prioritize an innovative, demanding curriculum and practical training coupled with on-the-ground research in our 7 laboratories.

    This hybrid approach connects knowledge to science, the academic to the professional, and enables us to bring together savoir-faire and skill to build a better future for us all.

    The ecological transition and the digital revolution
    are deeply ingrained in the university's programs and strategic focus.

    A pioneer in issues related to ecological and social transition on the one hand, and in the impact of AI on our society on the other, our university is developing two major programs: Sustainable Dauphine and Digital Dauphine. Through our instruction and our research centers, we draw on our institution's unique positioning, relying on cross-over expertise from several disciplines, including mathematics, computer science and humanities and organizational sciences to follow and light the way as our society undergoes deep-seated change.
